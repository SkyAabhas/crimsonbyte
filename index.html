About

CrimsonByte Interactive is an independent technology and game development company focused on creating immersive, story-driven digital experiences. Serving as the parent company, it oversees vision, technology, and long-term strategy, while its subsidiary, CrimsonForge Studios, handles core game development and creative execution. Together, they aim to build original, grounded, and meaningful games with a strong focus on storytelling and gameplay innovation.
Aabhas Dubey is the Managing Director (MD) of CrimsonByte Interactive, leading the company’s overall direction and growth.

Upcoming Project

Project Streets

Streets is an ambitious, narrative-driven game universe exploring power, ambition, and survival across interconnected stories. Each title within the Streets universe presents a grounded and intense perspective, creating a larger world shaped by choices, conflict, and consequence.

Currently Working On

Chaiwala Tycoon

Chaiwala Tycoon is a simulation-based game centered around building and scaling a chai business from a humble stall into a thriving enterprise. The project focuses on progression, strategy, and the everyday hustle, delivering a grounded and relatable gameplay experience.

CrimsonCore™

CrimsonCore™ is a proprietary C++-based game engine currently in development, built to power the Streets Universe and future in-house projects with a focus on performance, scalability, and creative freedom.
